What is Knomi?

Knomi was inspired by and created for holistic health. We feel that becoming healthier can take on many forms, be it emotional, physical, mental, or any other area of your life that needs improvement. In the pursuit of becoming a healthier individual, it can be hard to make changes in your life without upsetting the other parts of your life. It can also be difficult to know whether the changes you make are helping or hurting you. Knomi is a tool for those who want to be able to track and visualize their life changes.

How does Knomi work?

Knomi works as a personalized self-reporting tracking application inspired by some of the most helpful tracking apps out there. Knomi is an all-in-one way for you to track different parts of your life that may be factors in your overall health. You begin by picking and choosing things you'd like to keep track of over time. Would you like to watch your nutrition? You can input your calorie or macro-nutrient intake daily. Perhaps you may want to watch how exercising two times a week affects your daily emotions. Knomi will allow you to report your excersize sessions and rate how your feeling each day. You can even use Knomi as a personal journal.

Logging your life doesn't do much without being able to review it. Knomi will allow you to see reports that will inform you on how your changes are changing you over time. Useful statistics and graphs are provided so that you can plainly see how your behaviors or actions may be affecting you. Knomi allows you to make observations and understand trends in your life that help you know where you are and what you still want to change.
